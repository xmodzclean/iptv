How many simultaneous connections are allowed?

You can install your account on multiple devices, but keep in mind that it only works on one device at a time.

We recommend taking a speed test at speedtest.net. If your download speed is not less than 12 Mbps, it's good. If you still have doubts, try for 24 hours first.
